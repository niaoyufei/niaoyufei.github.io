隐私政策 | Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective Date：2026-04-02

欢迎使用 图框计 (FrameNote)。我们非常重视您的隐私，并致力于通过产品设计来保护您的个人信息。本政策说明了我们在您使用本移动应用程序时处理信息的方式。

Welcome to FrameNote. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information through product design. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. 核心理念：隐私第一 / Core Philosophy: Privacy First

“图框计”是一款专注于本地效率与审美的工具。我们秉承“沙盒级隔离”的理念，所有的影像处理逻辑均在您的设备本地完成。

FrameNote is a tool focused on local efficiency and aesthetics. We adhere to the concept of "sandbox-level isolation," meaning all image processing logic is completed locally on your device.

  • 100% 纯本地运行 / 100% Local Execution： 应用不需要连接互联网即可完成核心功能。
    The application does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions.
  • 无服务器上传 / No Server Uploads： 您的照片、相片元数据（EXIF）、排版配置及生成的导出图绝不会被上传到我们的任何服务器或第三方云端。
    Your photos, metadata (EXIF), layout configurations, and exported images are never uploaded to any of our servers or third-party clouds.

2. 信息收集与权限使用 / Information Collection and Permissions Usage

为了提供核心功能，我们需要申请以下权限，但我们绝不通过这些权限收集您的个人数据：

To provide core functions, we need to request the following permissions, but we absolutely do not use these permissions to collect your personal data:

  • 照片/相册访问权限 / Photo/Album Access Permission： 仅用于让您选择需要加框的照片，以及将生成的图片保存回相册。所有对照片的读取仅在您的设备内存中临时进行。
    Used solely to let you select photos to frame and to save the generated images back to your album. All photo reading is done temporarily within your device's memory.
  • EXIF 元数据读取 / EXIF Metadata Reading： 仅用于自动计算并显示相片所代表的“天数”刻度，这些信息仅保存在您的本地缓存中。
    Used solely to automatically calculate and display the "days" scale represented by the photo. This information is saved only in your local cache.

3. 信息存储与保护 / Information Storage and Protection

由于应用不收集任何个人身份信息（PII），我们不存储关于您的任何账户信息或行为数据。您在应用内的所有配置均保存在您的设备本地沙盒中，删除应用将导致这些本地缓存配置的丢失。

Because the application does not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), we do not store any account information or behavioral data about you. All your configurations within the app are saved in your device's local sandbox, and deleting the app will result in the loss of these local cache configurations.

4. 第三方 SDK 与服务 / Third-Party SDKs and Services

为保持产品的纯粹性，“图框计”目前不集成任何第三方广告跟踪或数据分析 SDK。我们致力于提供无干扰、无追踪的纯净使用环境。

To maintain the purity of the product, FrameNote currently does not integrate any third-party ad tracking or data analytics SDKs. We are committed to providing a pure, distraction-free, and tracking-free environment.
