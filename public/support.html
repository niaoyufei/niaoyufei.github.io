常见问题 / FAQ

Q: 为什么应用无法保存图片到相册？ / Why can't the app save images to my album?

A: 请确保您在首次启动或系统设置中授予了“图框计”访问照片图库的权限。您可以在 iOS 设置 -> 隐私与安全性 -> 照片中找到“图框计”并修改权限。
Please ensure you have granted FrameNote permission to access your photo library during the first launch or in your system settings. You can find "FrameNote" in iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Photos to modify the permissions.

Q: 时间戳计算不准确怎么办？ / What if the timestamp calculation is inaccurate?

A: “图框计”依赖照片原始的 EXIF 元数据。如果照片经过某些社交软件二次处理导致元数据丢失，应用可能无法读取准确日期。建议使用手机相册内的原图进行处理。
FrameNote relies on the original EXIF metadata of the photos. If a photo has been processed by certain social media platforms causing the metadata to be lost, the app may not be able to read the accurate date. It is recommended to use the original photos in your local album.

Q: 应用会收集我的照片吗？ / Will the app collect my photos?

A: 绝对不会。应用 100% 离线运行，所有处理逻辑均在您的设备本地完成。详情请参阅我们的 隐私政策。
Absolutely not. The app runs 100% offline, and all processing logic is done locally on your device. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
